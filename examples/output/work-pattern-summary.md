# Personal Work Pattern Summary

## Job In Plain English
I run a portfolio of AI enablement and transformation work that depends on synthesis, follow-through, and structured operating cadence. My time goes into collecting uneven inputs from many owners, turning them into useful views for leadership, and keeping cross-workstream commitments moving. The recurring work is highly pattern-based, but the judgment about what matters, what to escalate, and how to frame tradeoffs stays with me.

## What Shapes The Work
I am accountable for portfolio clarity, follow-through across multiple workstreams, and leadership-ready narratives about progress, blockers, and opportunities.

## Friction
The drag comes from inconsistent updates, context switching across programs, having to restitch the same story from multiple sources, and manually tracking follow-through across owners.

## What Should Stay Human
I keep the final tradeoff calls, relationship management, escalation timing, leadership messaging, and design judgment for new models or frameworks.

## What Stays With Me
Final tradeoff calls, relationship management, escalation timing, leadership messaging tone, and design judgment for new models. The 'what matters' and 'how to frame it' stay human — the 'collect it and organize it' can move to AI.
