# 3-Bucket Classification

Jen Weigel — Program manager and chief-of-staff style operator for AI enablement

## Bucket 1: AI Automates (AI does it)

Routine, repeatable, well-defined work. AI can handle these end-to-end with light review.

| Responsibility | Mode | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| Agentic Audience standup | automate-or-delegate | Strong candidate for end-to-end AI with a light approval gate. |
| Task and accountability operations | automate-or-delegate | Strong candidate for end-to-end AI with a light approval gate. |

## Bucket 2: AI Assists (AI + You)

AI drafts, assembles, or analyzes. You review, refine, and decide.

| Responsibility | Mode | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| KR 3.1 portfolio integration | assist-first | AI reduces prep, aggregation, and coordination load. You own synthesis and decisions. |
| Daily ops and triage | assist-first | AI reduces prep, aggregation, and coordination load. You own synthesis and decisions. |

## Bucket 3: Human (Only you)

Judgment, trust, ambiguity, presence. AI can prep, but you do the work.

| Responsibility | Mode | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly AI transformation narrative | human-led | Keep human-led. Use AI for note cleanup or targeted research only. |
| Cohort-based AI acceleration design | human-led | Keep human-led. Use AI for note cleanup or targeted research only. |
